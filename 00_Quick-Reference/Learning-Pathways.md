## üí° Learning Tips

### Effective Learning Strategies
1. **Active Learning:** Implement everything from scratch
2. **Spaced Repetition:** Review concepts regularly
3. **Project-Based:** Build real projects
4. **Teach Others:** Explain concepts to solidify understanding
5. **Read Papers:** Stay current with research

### Time Management
- **Daily:** 2-3 hours focused study
- **Weekly:** 1 project day
- **Monthly:** 1 comprehensive review

### Resources Mix
- **Theory:** 40% (reading, understanding)
- **Practice:** 40% (coding, implementing)
- **Projects:** 20% (applying knowledge)
